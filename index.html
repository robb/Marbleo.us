How to play

  • Drag blocks from the palette onto the map.
  • Add at least one block with a hole on top to the map.
  • Click “Test your Map!” to try out your map.

Share your creation!

Click “Share your Map!” to send your friends what you have built.

About us

Marbleo.us was build by Robert Böhnke and Simon Hohberg in 2011 for a students project at Freie Universität Berlin.

This project was built using CoffeeScript
